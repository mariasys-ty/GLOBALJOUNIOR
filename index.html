About Us

Welcome to Global Junior School Entebbe

A place where every child is nurtured to discover their potential and achieve excellence.

7+ Years of
Excellence

Building Futures, Transforming Lives

Global Junior School Entebbe is a leading co-educational institution committed to academic excellence, character development, and the holistic growth of every child. Established in 2019, we have grown into one of the most respected schools in Entebbe and Uganda at large.

Our blend of the Cambridge International Curriculum and the Uganda National Curriculum ensures students receive a globally competitive education rooted in local values — starting right from our vibrant Nursery section.

Early Childhood Learning Approach

We believe children learn best when they're engaged, happy, and exploring. Our play-based approach follows international ECD standards tailored to the Ugandan context.

Play-Based Learning

Structured and free play activities that develop cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills through hands-on experiences.

Storytelling Sessions

Daily storytelling that builds vocabulary, listening skills, imagination, and a love for books from an early age.

Music & Dance

Traditional Ugandan dances, songs, and rhythm instruments that celebrate culture while developing coordination and confidence.

Creative Arts & Crafts

Painting, clay modelling, collage, and craft activities that nurture creativity, fine motor skills, and self-expression.

Early Literacy

Phonics, letter recognition, sight words, and early reading activities that build a strong literacy foundation.

Early Numeracy

Counting, number recognition, patterns, sorting, and basic arithmetic through fun, interactive activities.

A Day in Our Nursery

Every day is carefully structured to balance learning, play, rest, and fun — creating a routine that children love and parents trust.

7:00 – 7:30 AM

Morning Welcome

Warm greeting at the gate, health check, and free play as children settle in. Parents hand over to teachers with a brief daily update.

7:30 – 9:30 AM

Learning Activities

Structured lessons in literacy, numeracy, and theme-based learning. Circle time, phonics, number games, and hands-on activities.

9:30 – 10:00 AM

Snack Break

Healthy snack time with fruits, milk, and nutritious treats. Children learn table manners and social eating.

10:00 – 11:30 AM

Playtime

Outdoor playground activities, organised games, music and dance, creative arts, and free exploration time.

11:30 AM – 12:30 PM

Lunch & Rest

Nutritious hot meal prepared by our school kitchen, followed by a supervised rest/nap time in the sleeping area.

12:30 – 1:30 PM

Storytelling & Quiet Activities

Interactive storytelling, picture reading, puzzles, and calm creative activities to wind down the day.

1:30 – 2:00 PM

Home Time

Children gather belongings, receive a goodbye song, and are handed over to parents/guardians or placed on the school bus with proper identification.

Nursery Admissions

Enrolling your child is simple and straightforward. Follow the steps below or contact our admissions team for personal assistance.

Age Requirements

Baby Class 2 – 3 years Born 2022 – 2023
Middle Class 3 – 4 years Born 2021 – 2022
Top Class 4 – 5 years Born 2020 – 2021

Enrollment Process

  1. Visit the School

    Book a campus tour to see our nursery facilities and meet the teachers.

  2. Collect Application Form

    Pick up from the school office or download online.

  3. Submit Documents

    Completed form, birth certificate, 4 passport photos, immunisation card.

  4. Child Assessment

    A friendly, play-based interaction to understand your child's development level.

  5. Confirmation & Payment

    Pay the required fees to secure your child's place.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of child's birth certificate
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • Immunisation/health card
  • Copy of parent/guardian ID
  • 2 passport photos of each parent/guardian
